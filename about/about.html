Together, We Create a Safer and Healthier World

The Integrated Agenda for Bacterial Infection Control Foundation is dedicated to reducing the burden of bacterial infections through science, collaboration, and education. We bring together researchers, healthcare professionals, and communities to implement effective infection prevention strategies and promote sustainable health practices across all levels of society.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to build a society where every community embraces good hygiene as a way of life. We strive to make cleanliness a daily culture by promoting bacterial infection prevention through education, sanitation programs, and behavioural change advocacy. Through collective awareness and sustained community engagement, we aim to create healthier environments and empower individuals to take responsibility for their well-being and that of others.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to prevent bacterial infections and promote hygiene education by supporting clean sanitation infrastructures and inspiring lasting behavioral change across schools and communities. We are dedicated to empowering individuals with the knowledge and resources to practice effective hygiene, reduce disease transmission, and foster environments where health, safety, and well-being become shared community values.

WHAT INSPIRES EVERYTHING WE DO

AWARENESS

Knowledge is the first step toward prevention. By spreading awareness about bacterial infections, we empower individuals and communities to take action for better health.

CARE & LOVE

Compassion drives every initiative we take. Our work is rooted in genuine care and love for people, ensuring that every life is valued, protected, and supported.

COMMITMENT

We are dedicated to building a healthier world through consistent action, scientific rigor, and collaboration with those who share our mission to stop preventable infections.

SUSTAINABILITY

Our programs are designed for lasting impact creating systems that strengthen healthcare practices, promote hygiene, and sustain community well-being for generations.

MEET THE FOUNDER OF IABIC

Miss Celia Emehuru Obinna , a visionary woman from Rundele in Emohua Local Government Area Of Rivers State, passionate about ending poor hygiene conditions and bacterial infections in schools and communities in Rivers State and across Nigeria. She attended the Government Girls Secondary School Rumueme Port Harcourt Rivers State, formerly known as Model Girls Secondary School and lived in the school environment as a boarder in the school’s hostel. Miss Celia Emehuru Obinna founded the integrated agenda for bacterial infection control. She is a passionate advocate for public health, hygiene and child wellbeing. Inspired by the poor sanitary conditions of public Schools and the alarming rate of bacterial infections, she acted founding IABIC on August 23 2023.

Every Child Deserves A Clean And Safe Toilet

Through her leadership, IABIC has become a beacon for better sanitation and
hygiene practices in Nigeria. In this regard she wrote a book titled Good
Hygiene, Good Living. This book acts as a guide for students in secondary
schools, on how bacteria infections can be avoided, how to manage the
cleanliness of toilets and how maintaining a proper hygiene can influence their
environment. She understands the need for having good toilet facilities in
schools as she herself attended a public school during her teenage years and
she has made it her mission to support students and communities with her
knowledge and experiences.
